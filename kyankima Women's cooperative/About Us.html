About Us

Our Story

Kyankima Women's Cooperative is a registered community-based organization in Mukono District, Uganda, Founded in the 2019 by 47 women involved in small-scale agriculture, handcraft production, and Community Health awareness.

Meet The Team


Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering women through economic opportunities and skill development.
  • Collaboration: We work together as a community to achieve common goals and support one another.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices that benefit both our members and the environment.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have provided training and resources to our members, enabling them to improve their livelihoods and contribute to their communities. We have also organized health workshops and community events to promote well-being and social cohesion.

CERTIFICATES

We are proud to have received several certificates of recognition for our

contributions to the community, including awards for our agricultural practices and handcraft production. These certificates reflect our dedication to excellence and our commitment to making a positive impact in Mukono.
